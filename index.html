Biography

I am the eldest of a first generation family and the first in my extended family to ever attend college. I was born in Guanajuato, Mexico. My love for math and genuine passion for the community propelled me into such an accomplishment. However, it was my commitment to building a better world for my family and my community, through a mission-driven career, that encouraged my mentors to introduce me to engineering. The curiosity, imagination, love for learning and the success with hands-on environments both as a child and as an adult led me to dive into the challenge. I now have two degrees, teaching and mechanical engineering. These degrees make me a qualified and passionate graduate of Seattle University seeking an opportunity that aligns with my mission and vision as an engineer.
